Hi there! I'm Edward and I'm passionate about AI safety and alignment research. I think unaligned AI, AGI or not, poses many threats to humanity. Threats that may not be intially obvious and threats that we are unable to recover from. When I'm not geeking out about AI, you'll often find me surfing, hiking, cycling, playing tennis, or strategizing my next move on the GO board.

I'm aware of the privilege I have in life, and I feel a sense of responsibility to use it to make a positive impact. I believe that humor and creativity can help us think outside the box and come up with novel solutions to complex problems.
